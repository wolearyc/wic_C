Detailed Description

Stores two double precision floating point values.

Pair is the workhorse of the Wick Engine. Pair objects are used to represent two dimensional vectors, dimensions, scaling parameters, and, most commonly, x-y coordinates. The usual mathematical operations and comparisons can be performed between Pairs, as well as angle and distance formulas (assuming the two Pairs represent x-y coordinates.
